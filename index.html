Welcome to Daly's Hardware Store in Limerick City

Limerick's leading provider of DIY, hardware, home decor, lighting & electrical goods and services!

Dalys Hardware Store of Limerick city has been serving the people of Limerick City and beyond since 1958. We pride ourselves in offering an extensive selection of quality household goods and DIY products and materials at affordable prices - always with the superior customer service for which we have become renowned.

We only stock top quality DIY products and brands to assist you choose the right tool for the job, from a drill set to a power saw, and everything inbetween that you might need to decorated or improve your home or garden.

We pride ourselves on looking after our customers. If you have any questions or need advice, our dedicated team is here to help. With new stock arriving each week, customers can keep an eye out for our regular special offers in-store.
